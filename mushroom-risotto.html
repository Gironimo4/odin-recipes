Mushroom Risotto

Giron mushroom risotto is a staple of holiday meals. It's the perfect vessel for cheese to reach the stomach.

  • Vegetable Stock
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • White Wine
  • Arborio Rice
  • Shallots/White Onions
  1. *Optional* heat vegetable stock on a separate burner
  2. Sweat onions, garlic, and butter together in a sauce pan
  3. Add 1 cup of Arborio Rice to the sauce pan
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable stock to the rice
  5. Repeat every 3-5 minutes or whenever stock evaporates
  6. When rice reaches desired doneness (typically 7-8 repetitions of previous step), add mushrooms and white wine
  7. When wine evaporates, add heavy cream and parmesan cheese
  8. Stir for an additional 5 minutes and then remove from heat
  9. Serve hot or allow to cool
